About Our School

A legacy of learning, built on faith, discipline, and community.

Saint Mary School, Rajula was founded in 1985 with a singular mission — to provide quality education rooted in strong values to the children of Rajula and surrounding regions of Amreli district, Gujarat.

Affiliated with the Gujarat Secondary and Higher Secondary Education Board (GSEB), the school offers comprehensive education from Standard I to XII. Our campus is home to well-equipped classrooms, modern science and computer laboratories, a rich library, and dedicated sports grounds.

Over nearly four decades, Saint Mary has produced thousands of alumni who excel in medicine, engineering, civil services, arts, and beyond — all carrying the hallmarks of discipline, integrity, and compassion.
